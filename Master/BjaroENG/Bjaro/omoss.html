Who are we?

We are, Roseanne, Daniella and André, a completely normal family living in Sundsvall, Sweden.

The reason for first adding another member to our family is because of my daughter's huge interest in cats. When we began looking into getting one we realized that Daniella is allergic to cats. During a period of time we tried diffrent animals such including diffrent types of fish and birds.

But she wanted something she could cuddle with, and a turtle is a bit rough to snuggle up to. When a classmate of Daniella got a siberian can and spoke of how allergy friendly they are we decided to try anew!

Shortly we visited Eva Gunnarsson, where Daniella got to try getting snuggly with all her cats, and miraculously she didn't show any signs of allergy, neither there or at home.

We were hooked, this breed is amazing!

In conjuncture with a cat show in Njurunda, outside of Sundsvall, we got into contact with Monica Byström who's cat had a litter on the way.

When the kittens were here we chose Silvia (S*Kneph's Silver Fizz). A more incredible cat is hard to find.

On the 14th of January 2005 our family grew from four to five members when Silvia joined us.
